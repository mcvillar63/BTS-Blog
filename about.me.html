BTS Family




BTS is a 7 member group, but they are like family. They have known each other

for 11 years and have lived in the same quarters, sharing a room with each other

since their pre-debut days. They know each other's biological families, have

shared holiday celebrations, and go on trips together.




김석진 (Kim Seok-jin)

Jin is the oldest member (31), born on December 4th, 1993. He is a vocalist and has writing credits on BTS' albums. He is said to have a Golden ratio face (his face is perfection).

민윤기 (Min Yoongi)

정호석(Jung Hoseok)

Yoongi was born on March 9th, 1993. He is one of three rappers in BTS. He started his rapping career in the underground Korean Hip Hop/Rap scene. Hoseok was born on February 18th, 1994. He is also a rapper, but he is most known for his amazing dancing abilities.

김남준 (Kim Namjoon)

박지민 (Park Jimin)

Namjoon was born on September 2nd, 1994. He is the third rapper and leader of BTS. He gave a speach at the United Nations in 2018. Jimin was born on October 13th, 1995. He is one of dancer of BTS. He began his dancing career as a comtemporary dancer.

김태형 (Kim Taehyung)

전정국 (Jeon Jugkook)

Tae was born on December 30th, 1995. He is a vocailist and has the deepest voice in BTS. He is a fan of Jazz and has produced Jazz songs. Jungkook (JK) was born on September 1st, 1997. He is a vocalist.He is known as the 'Golden' member because he is good at everything (singing, sports, gaming).
