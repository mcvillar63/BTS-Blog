The Beginning

BTS was formed by Bang Si-Hyuk, who established his own Entertainment Company, Big Hit Entertainment in South Korea. BTS, Bulletproof Boy Scouts as they were first known, was started in 2010. The first member was Kim Namjoon who was scouted when he was part of the underground rap scene.

Rise to Fame

BTS was not the most popular boy band group when they first began in South Korea. Their first fan meet was only attended by a few fans, called ARMY. They slowly grew their popularity, particularly standing out with their energenitc performances. Their first American Billiboard Award in 2017 (for Top Social Artist) solidified their worlwide popularity and power.

Worlwide Artists

They are now known as worlwide artists! They have received accolades in both their home countries and accross the world. BTS has also collaborate with artists around the world, in different languages. They keep breaking world records as a group and as independent artists. All members have now released independent albums despite some members serving in the South Korean army. Here's to more from BTS!

Did you know that ...

BTS have accrued 23 Guinness Word Rcords. They have even beaten Coldplay as the most streamed group in Spotify and have the most followers on Instagram.
